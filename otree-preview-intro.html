Selecting your move

You are the row player in a stag-hunt game. On each round you choose between Stag and Hare.

The column player simultaneously chooses Stag or Hare. Neither of you sees the other's choice before deciding. Your payoff in points depends on both choices, as shown below.

Payoffs (row player, column player)

Row: Stag 4, 4 0, 3
Row: Hare 3, 0 3, 3
