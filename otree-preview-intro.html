Selecting your move

You are the row player in a stag-hunt game. On each round you choose between Stag and Hare.

A second participant — the column player — simultaneously chooses Stag or Hare. Neither of you sees the other's choice before deciding. Your payoff in points depends on both choices, as shown in the table below.

Payoffs (row player, column player)
Column: Stag Column: Hare
Row: Stag 4, 4 0, 3
Row: Hare 3, 0 3, 3

Stag pays best when both of you choose it, but pays nothing if you alone go for it. Hare pays a steady amount no matter what the column player does.
