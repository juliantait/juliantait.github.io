About Me

I am a third-year PhD candidate in Behavioural Economics at the University of Amsterdam, affiliated with CREED and the Tinbergen Institute. I am supervised by Randolph Sloof and Jeroen van de Ven.

My research uses experimental methods to explore how reputation concerns impact communication — such as giving advice or admitting mistakes in teams. I am also interested in applying these methods to behaviour with an environmental component.
