About Me

I’m a PhD student in Behavioural Economics at the University of Amsterdam working under the supervision of Randolph Sloof and Jeroen van de Ven. I apply experimental methods to explore how reputation concerns impact communication; such as giving advice or admitting mistakes in teams. I am interested in applying these methods to behaviour with an environmental component.
