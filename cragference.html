CRAG-ference

CRAG-ference connects climbing academics at conferences. Travelling for a conference often means a new city with great crags or gyms nearby — but no one to climb with. CRAG-ference fixes that:

  • Share which conferences you are attending
  • Say what you want to climb while you are there
  • Find others to climb with at the same event
Add to your website?
